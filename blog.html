📝 Why We Built CTRL₹ – The Budget App for the Broke Yet Brilliant Gen Z

By Team CTRL₹ · JULY 2025

Let's be real.
We're a generation that spends more time on Swiggy than spreadsheets, and keeping track of where our money goes feels like solving an IIT paper blindfolded.

That's exactly why CTRL₹ was born.

We didn't want just another boring budget app.
We wanted something that actually talks to us — visually, simply, and without judgment.
An app that understands what it feels like to survive on Maggi, yet still dream of saving for that Goa trip.

💡 So what is CTRL₹?

  • CTRL₹ (Control Rupee) is a daily, weekly, and monthly expense tracker built for Gen Z and college students who:
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Forget where their money went
  • 📒 Hate writing expenses in notebooks
  • 🎨 Want fun UI with actual useful data
  • 🚨 Need alerts before they go broke again 😅

🧠 What makes CTRL₹ different?

✔️ Spending Meter HUD – Like a game UI. Shows how much you can still spend.
✔️ Category-wise breakdown – Food, travel, dates, Zomato, everything.
✔️ Smart Filters – Track by day, week, or month with just one tap.

🚀 Why we're building this (and what's coming)

This app isn't just a project — it's a mission to help young people take control without feeling controlled.

Coming soon:

  • 💡 Budget tips & hacks for students
  • 📈 Daily stats and trends
  • 🤖 Smart suggestions based on your spending style
We believe being broke is a phase. But being in control? That's a mindset.
And CTRL₹ is here to make that mindset easier to live by.

🔔 Stay Tuned

We're still building. Still improving. Still broke (but in control 😉).
